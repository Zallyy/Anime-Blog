Hello I'm Zally, I made this website mainly as practice, but also as an outlet for all my anime-related thoughts. I've been watching anime since I was 12 years old, and it's left a big impact on my life. I used to think talking about it and expressing myself was a little cringe, but even if it is, it's so darn fun. Anime is my main, but not only inspiration, to me, nothing feels better than watching an episode of your favorite show while you're down. I hope you enjoy just looking around, this isn't meant to be super serious, but if you find some meaning in it, that's cool too.
