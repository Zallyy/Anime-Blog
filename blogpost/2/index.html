My Favorite Openings~

Note: This will be an updated entry for myself for easy access.

Barakamon

This opening, and in turn, this entire show makes me so happy. Watching Handa learn to let loose and have fun was an eye opener to me, since I myself can get caught up in the seriousness of it all. We all need a reminder to enjoy the little things around us.

Aria The Animation

This song + show combo never fail to relax me and put me at ease. Despite how cheesy the show can be at times, it makes me want to be the best person I can be.

Madoka Magica

I really want to rewatch this show, when I first watched it, I didn't really care too much about it to be honest, I feel like I'll have a new appreciation now that I'm older. Anyways, this opening is a banger.

New Game!!

Umm.. I don't really know what I like about this one... I guess it's catchy and cute. 0:48 That smile probably
