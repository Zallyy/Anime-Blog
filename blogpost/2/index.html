My Favorite Openings~

12-11-2020

Note: This will be an updated entry for myself for easy access.

Barakamon

This opening, and in turn, this entire show makes me so happy. Watching Handa learn to let loose and have fun was an eye opener to me, since I myself can get caught up in the seriousness of it all. We all need a reminder to enjoy the little things around us.

Soul Eater

I absolutely love this opening, the visuals, the song, the atmosphere. If I had to make a list of the best openings in terms of sheer quality, this one would be up there.

Aria The Animation

This song + show combo never fail to relax me and put me at ease. Despite how cheesy the show can be at times, it makes me want to be the best person I can be.

Madoka Magica

I really want to rewatch this show, when I first watched it, I didn't really care too much about it to be honest, I feel like I'll have a new appreciation now that I'm older. Anyways, this opening is a banger.

New Game!!

Umm.. I don't really know what I like about this one... I guess it's catchy and cute. 0:48 That smile probably

Serial Experiments Lain

This is the anime that really got me more interested in the cyber-punk genre. It was also my first physical purchase of an anime series. I hold it near and dear, and I still do in the Present day, and present time

Guilty Crown

I don't particularly care for this show, but the music is on point and it's beautiful.

Btooom!

No pain no gain, when I first watched this show, I could not get this song out of my head. And I still thing it deserves a spot on my favorites

Shakugan No Shana III

This was one of my first anime, and although i'm sure I would think it's pretty basic now. I still think the openings in this show were killer. This show has mainly stuck with me for these reasons, also I just really like Shana's voice actor.
